BigTricks was built to make saving money straightforward for users during online shopping and utility payments. The product combined affiliate offers, cashback accounting, and campaign-focused merchandising into one flow.

A key challenge was staying stable under campaign spikes. We optimized bottlenecks around offers, redirects, and cashback tracking to keep response times predictable during peak windows.

The project established the foundation for multiple follow-up products and helped shape a practical architecture for high-traffic commerce events.
