CashBig was designed around reliability and trust in reward tracking. Every user action in the journey needed clean attribution to ensure cashback correctness.

The platform architecture emphasized stable integrations with partner networks and clean interfaces between frontend presentation and backend reconciliation.

This created a foundation for shipping offer updates quickly without compromising reporting and payout confidence.
